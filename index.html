Kheya Global Foundation is Nonprofit Organization For Help .

Kheyaa is Determined to ensure child rights and education, to provide peaceful livelihood for the distressed elders and to restore lives of the street dwellers through various specialised projects.

Regular campaigns to promote human rights, health and cleanliness, life and moral education, communal unity and universal brotherhood and culture are the integrated parts of its projects.
